Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Freelancer Take-Home Pay ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

No Data Collection

The Freelancer Take-Home Pay app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your income information
  • We do not collect your tax filing status
  • We do not collect your state of residence
  • We do not collect any other personal or financial information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Local Calculations

All tax calculations are performed entirely on your device. Your data never leaves your phone. We have no access to any information you enter into the calculator.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not share any information with external parties because we do not collect any information.

Security

Since all calculations occur locally on your device and no data is transmitted, your information is inherently secure. We do not maintain any servers or databases containing user data.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
